Quick Definitions

Campaign
One promo push or sequence sent to a defined group of members via any channel.
Outbound Marketing
Proactive promotions or messages designed to generate PT demand — not just conversations when members walk up and ask.
First PT Session
The paid or free first personal training session used to start a new member on PT.
Found First-Session Revenue
In a normal month without a campaign, maybe 3 members sign up for their first PT session. You run a campaign and 8 members sign up. The 5 extra sign-ups? That's your Found First-Session Revenue — the money your campaign actually created.
Package Revenue
Training package dollars collected from members who bought a package after a campaign-driven first PT session (typically within 30 days).
